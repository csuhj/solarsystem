The Solar System

×

Sun
The sun is the centre of the solar system and is Earth's main source of light. All the planets orbit around the Sun.

Moon
The moon orbits around the Earth, giving us months and lunar eclipses.

Mercury
Mercury is the closest planet to the sun and is made of rock.

Venus
Venus is almost exactly like Earth and is the second closest planet to the sun.

Earth
Earth is where we live and is the only planet with life on it (that we know of!!!).

Mars
Mars is very small and is one of Earth's neighbours, being the fourth closest planet to the sun.

Jupiter
Jupiter is the biggest planet in the solar system and has a whopping 79 moons according to NASA.

Saturn
Saturn is the planet with the biggest and brightest rings and is the second largest planet in the solar system.

Uranus
Uranus is a pale blue coloured planet with rings and its years are 30,688 days long!

Neptune
Neptune is the furthest planet from the sun and is also blue coloured.
